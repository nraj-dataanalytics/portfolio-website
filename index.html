About Me

I am a Business Analytics professional who works across the entire data lifecycle — from raw data and architecture to analysis, visualization, and decision-making. My work focuses on helping businesses make confident, data-driven decisions by turning complex datasets into clear, actionable insights.

I approach analytics with a strong balance of technical depth and business understanding. Whether I am designing databases, building predictive models, or creating executive dashboards, my focus is always on impact — ensuring the analysis aligns with real business problems, not just technical outcomes.

I enjoy working at the intersection of data, strategy, and execution. From dashboards to databases, forecasting to customer analytics, I aim to build solutions that are scalable, reliable, and easy for stakeholders to trust and use.
